About Bluestone University

Bluestone university

Founded in 2002, BlueStone University began as a small institution dedicated to providing quality education and practical learning. Named after the rare blue stones found in the region, the university symbolizes strength, wisdom, and excellence.

Initially starting with just three departments and a handful of students, BlueStone University quickly grew due to its innovative teaching methods, industry collaborations, and student-centered approach. By 2015, it had expanded to include multiple faculties, offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs in fields like Engineering, Business, Computer Science, Arts, and Healthcare.

Today, BlueStone University is known for its modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong alumni network. It continues to uphold its vision of empowering students with knowledge and skills for the future.
